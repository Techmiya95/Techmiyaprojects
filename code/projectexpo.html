All India Hackathon 2025

Get ready to showcase your innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills in the All India Hackathon 2025, hosted by Techmiya Solutions in collaboration with Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering, Kumaraswamy Layout, Bengauru. This event brings together the brightest minds from across the country to collaborate, compete, and create ground-breaking solutions.

Points to Remember:

  • A Group can have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 students.
  • A Group should contain students from the same department and college.
  • A Group can have students from different semesters.
  • File size of the Synopsis should be less than 10 MB.
